Neighborhood - Hell's Kitchen

Hell's Kitchen

Clinton is an diverse neighborhood just west of the Theater District. Despite several waves of gentrification that have raised rents and brought increased numbers of young professionals to the neighborhood, Clinton retains some of the grit for which it was known when its only name was Hell's Kitchen, and its low-rise nature makes it unique in Manhattan. With high-end restaurants and proximity to the Hudson River, Central Park and the theaters of Broadway, Clinton is an exciting and welcoming residential neighborhood.
